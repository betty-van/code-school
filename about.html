About Us

Here are Code School, we are dedicated to your learning. We were founded on the belief that free education for all is a right, not a privilege.

In 2019, we started as a group of four people trying to learn how to program with no prior knowledge. We crowded around a table at a cafe as our initial starting point.

The question we asked was: Where do we start?

If this question has ran through your mind when you first started, let us help you pick a path.

As a learning program, we primarily focus on Javascript. It is a high-level, object-oriented scripting language that allows us to write code that will shape the way your websites and web apps will run.
